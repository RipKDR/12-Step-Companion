### 1. **"Streak Insurance" - Protect Your Progress**
**Concept**: Users can "insure" their streak for a small commitment (e.g., completing a future task)

**How It Works**:
- User has a 30-day streak
- App offers: "Protect your streak! Complete 3 daily entries this week and your streak is protected for 7 days"
- If user misses a day, streak is "restored" once (one-time use)
- Creates psychological investment - users don't want to "waste" their insurance

**Why It Works**: Loss aversion - people work harder to avoid losing something than to gain something. Protecting a streak feels more valuable than building one.

**Implementation**:
- Earn "Streak Protection" tokens by completing routines
- Can "activate" protection when streak is at risk
- One-time use per token
- Creates forward commitment

### 2. **"Future Self Letter" - Time-Locked Messages**
**Concept**: Users write letters to their future self that unlock at milestones

**How It Works**:
- User writes letter to "Future Me at 90 Days"
- Letter is encrypted and locked
- Only unlocks when milestone is reached
- Can include photos, voice notes, video messages
- Can write multiple letters for different milestones

**Why It Works**: 
- Creates emotional investment in reaching milestones
- Provides motivation during difficult times
- Personal, meaningful reward (not generic)
- Creates anticipation and excitement

**Implementation**:
- Write letter at any time
- Set milestone (30, 60, 90 days, etc.)
- Can preview "teaser" but full content locked
- Unlocks automatically at milestone
- Can share with sponsor (optional)

### 3. **"Commitment Contract" - Public or Private Pledges**
**Concept**: Users create commitment contracts with consequences/rewards

**How It Works**:
- User creates contract: "I commit to 30 days of daily entries"
- Choose accountability: Sponsor, Recovery Circle, or Private
- Set reward: "If I complete this, I'll treat myself to [X]"
- Set consequence: "If I don't complete this, I'll [meaningful action]"
- Contract is locked and visible to accountability partners

**Why It Works**: 
- Public commitment increases follow-through by 65% (research)
- Pre-commitment reduces decision fatigue
- Creates external accountability
- Makes goals concrete and actionable

**Implementation**:
- Create contract with specific goals
- Choose accountability level (private, sponsor, circle)
- Set meaningful rewards/consequences
- Track progress toward contract
- Celebrate completion or handle consequences

### 4. **"Recovery Investment Portfolio" - Visualize Your Investment**
**Concept**: Users "invest" time/effort and see it grow like a financial portfolio

**How It Works**:
- Each daily entry = $1 invested
- Each meeting attended = $5 invested
- Each step completed = $10 invested
- "Portfolio" grows over time
- Can see "returns": improved mood, longer streaks, etc.
- Visual chart shows "portfolio value" over time

**Why It Works**: 
- Sunk cost fallacy - users don't want to "lose" their investment
- Visual progress is motivating
- Makes abstract effort concrete
- Creates sense of ownership

**Implementation**:
- Track all recovery activities
- Assign "investment values" to activities
- Visual portfolio chart
- Show "returns" (correlations with outcomes)
- Can "withdraw" (relapse) but shows impact
