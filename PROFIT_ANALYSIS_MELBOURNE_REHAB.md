## Risk Factors & Mitigation

### **Risk 1: App Completion Delays**
- **Probability**: Medium (30-40%)
- **Impact**: Delayed revenue, missed rehab opening partnership
- **Mitigation**:
  - Launch MVP with core features, iterate post-launch
  - Use your current codebase (already 70-80% complete)
  - Cut non-essential features for v1.0
  - Timeline buffer: Aim for Month 1.5 launch

### **Risk 2: Rehab Partnership Falls Through**
- **Probability**: Low-Medium (20-30%)
- **Impact**: Loss of primary B2B revenue ($5-10k)
- **Mitigation**:
  - Diversify: Don't rely on single B2B client
  - Build relationships with multiple facilities
  - Focus on proven clinical outcomes
  - Have backup: Individual subscriptions still work

### **Risk 3: Low NA Community Adoption**
- **Probability**: Medium (30-40%)
- **Impact**: Lower user base, fewer premium subscriptions
- **Mitigation**:
  - Active meeting attendance and networking
  - Sponsor endorsements and word-of-mouth
  - Excellent UX and retention features
  - Provide real value (free tier must be excellent)

### **Risk 4: User Retention Failure**
- **Probability**: High if not addressed (60-70%)
- **Impact**: High churn, poor word-of-mouth, no B2B renewals
- **Mitigation**:
  - **Implement all critical retention features** from research
  - Daily engagement mechanisms (notifications, streaks)
  - Excellent onboarding (value in < 2 minutes)
  - Sponsor connection features
  - Continuous user feedback loop

### **Risk 5: AOD Job + App = Burnout**
- **Probability**: Medium (30-40%)
- **Impact**: Poor performance in both, health issues
- **Mitigation**:
  - Set clear boundaries (app work: 10-15 hrs/week max)
  - Automate where possible (CI/CD, monitoring)
  - Build sustainable systems, not constant hustle
  - Prioritize sleep and recovery

### **Risk 6: Competitor Launches Similar App**
- **Probability**: Low-Medium (20-30%)
- **Impact**: Market share loss, pricing pressure
- **Mitigation**:
  - First-mover advantage in Melbourne NA community
  - Strong relationships (network effects)
  - Superior features (comprehensive 12-step focus)
  - Privacy-first positioning (unique value prop)
