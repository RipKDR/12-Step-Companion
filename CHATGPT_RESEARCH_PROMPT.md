# ChatGPT Research Prompt for 12-Step Recovery Companion App

## Copy-Paste Prompt:

```
I'm building a privacy-first 12-Step Recovery Companion mobile app (React Native/Expo) that helps people in recovery from addiction. The app includes features for NA/AA step work, daily recovery logging, action plans, sponsor connection, meeting finder, and crisis support.

I need you to research and provide evidence-based ideas and features for the following areas:

1. **User Engagement & Retention Strategies**
   - What features drive long-term app usage in recovery/mental health apps?
   - What causes users to abandon recovery apps (beyond the standard reasons)?
   - Unique gamification strategies that respect recovery (not trivializing it)?
   - Behavioral economics principles that increase engagement?
   - What makes users come back daily vs. abandoning after first use?

2. **Relapse Prevention & Staying Clean**
   - Evidence-based relapse prevention techniques beyond CBT and MBRP?
   - Innovative craving management strategies?
   - Early warning systems for relapse detection?
   - Just-in-time intervention strategies?
   - What coping skills are most effective for different types of cravings?
   - How can technology detect relapse risk before the user is aware?

3. **Crisis Intervention & Emergency Support**
   - Best practices for crisis intervention in mobile apps?
   - How to make emergency features always accessible?
   - What makes crisis support effective vs. ineffective?
   - Integration with crisis hotlines and emergency services?
   - How to reduce barriers to accessing help during crisis?

4. **Social Support & Accountability**
   - Evidence-based approaches to sponsor-sponsee relationships?
   - How to facilitate peer support without compromising privacy?
   - What makes accountability systems effective?
   - How to build trust in recovery communities?
   - Anonymous support mechanisms that work?

5. **Trigger Management & Avoidance**
   - Advanced trigger identification techniques?
   - How to help users avoid triggers proactively?
   - Geofencing and location-based interventions?
   - Environmental trigger management strategies?
   - How to handle triggers that can't be avoided?

6. **Mindfulness & Coping Skills**
   - Evidence-based mindfulness techniques for recovery?
   - What coping skills are most effective for different situations?
   - How to teach coping skills through an app?
   - Breathing exercises and grounding techniques beyond basics?
   - How to make coping skills "sticky" (users actually use them)?

7. **Meeting Integration & 12-Step Support**
   - How to make meeting attendance easier and more engaging?
   - Virtual meeting integration best practices?
   - Pre-meeting and post-meeting support?
   - How to help users get the most out of meetings?
   - Meeting finder features that actually get used?

8. **Step Work & Recovery Progress**
   - How to make step work engaging and meaningful?
   - Version control and progress tracking for step work?
   - How to help users reflect on step work effectively?
   - What makes step work completion more likely?
   - Integration of step work with daily recovery?

9. **Privacy & Security in Recovery Apps**
   - Privacy concerns specific to recovery apps?
   - How to build trust through privacy features?
   - Encryption and security best practices for sensitive recovery data?
   - How to balance privacy with support/accountability?
   - What privacy features do users in recovery actually want?

10. **Accessibility & Inclusion**
    - Accessibility features critical for recovery apps?
    - How to make the app usable during crisis (high stress, impaired cognition)?
    - Support for users with disabilities in recovery?
    - Multi-language and cultural considerations?
    - How to reduce barriers to app usage?

11. **Offline Functionality & Reliability**
    - What features MUST work offline for a recovery app?
    - How to ensure reliability during crisis situations?
    - Data sync strategies that don't compromise privacy?
    - How to handle offline/online transitions gracefully?

12. **Personalization & AI/ML**
    - How can AI/ML help with relapse prevention (privacy-preserving)?
    - Personalization strategies that respect recovery?
    - Predictive analytics for relapse risk?
    - How to personalize without being invasive?
    - What data is actually useful for personalization?

13. **Onboarding & First-Time User Experience**
    - What makes onboarding effective for recovery apps?
    - How to provide immediate value in first session?
    - How to reduce barriers to getting started?
    - What information is essential vs. optional?
    - How to make first use feel safe and supportive?

14. **Notifications & Reminders**
    - What types of notifications are helpful vs. annoying?
    - How to use notifications for relapse prevention?
    - Timing and frequency best practices?
    - How to make notifications feel supportive, not nagging?
    - Context-aware notification strategies?

15. **Data Visualization & Progress Tracking**
    - What progress metrics are most motivating?
    - How to visualize recovery progress effectively?
    - What data visualizations help prevent relapse?
    - How to show progress without creating pressure?
    - What makes progress tracking meaningful vs. gamified?

For each area, please provide:
- Research-backed findings and statistics
- Specific feature ideas with evidence
- What works vs. what doesn't (based on research)
- Unique/innovative approaches
- Implementation considerations
- Potential pitfalls to avoid
- Citations to research papers or studies when possible

Focus on evidence-based approaches, unique ideas beyond standard features, and practical implementation strategies. Please be thorough and cite sources where possible.
```

## Tips for Using These Prompts:

1. **Start Broad**: Use the full prompt first to get comprehensive coverage
2. **Then Focus**: Use focused prompts to dive deeper into specific areas
3. **Ask Follow-ups**: "Tell me more about [specific topic]" or "What research supports [specific idea]?"
4. **Request Citations**: "Can you provide citations for these findings?"
5. **Ask for Implementation**: "How would I implement [specific feature]?"
6. **Compare Approaches**: "Compare [approach A] vs. [approach B] based on research"
